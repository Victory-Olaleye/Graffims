Common Questions

Before You Contact Us

Check whether your question is already answered here — it may save you the wait.

How do I apply for admission?

Visit our Admissions page for the full step-by-step process, requirements, and to fill in the online application form.

Where do I pay school fees?

Fees are paid at the Bursar's office during working hours, or by bank transfer to our official school account. Details are provided in the offer letter.

How do I check my child's results?

End-of-term results are shared directly with parents at the school office and by post. Please contact the class teacher or Vice Principal for progress updates between terms.

Can I schedule a school tour?

Absolutely. Prospective families are welcome to visit during school hours. Call or email the Admissions Office to arrange a guided tour with a member of the team.

How do I raise a complaint?

We take all feedback seriously. Use the contact form above (select "Feedback" as the subject) or speak directly with the Vice Principal — Administration in person.

How do I submit photos or news for the website?

Send event photos and school news to graffimscollege@gmail.com with the event name and date. Our team will review and upload approved content.
