Our Curriculum

From Foundation to Final Exams

Our curriculum follows the Nigerian national curriculum framework, enriched with additional programmes to ensure every student reaches their full potential.

Primary School

Building Strong Foundations

Our primary school programme lays the essential groundwork for lifelong learning. We focus on literacy, numeracy, creativity, and character development in a warm, nurturing environment where every child is encouraged to explore and grow.

Class sizes are kept small to ensure personalised attention, and our teachers use a blend of structured lessons and play-based learning to keep young minds engaged and curious.

Small class sizes for personalised learning
Phonics-based literacy and early numeracy
Weekly creative arts, music, and physical education
Regular parent progress reports each term

Junior Secondary

Broadening Horizons

JSS builds on primary foundations with a broader, more structured curriculum. Students begin to explore a wider range of subjects and develop the study habits, critical thinking, and academic discipline that will carry them through senior school.

The three-year JSS programme culminates in the Basic Education Certificate Examination (BECE), for which our students are rigorously prepared by experienced subject specialists.

Subject specialist teachers for all core subjects
BECE preparation from JSS 2 onward
Introduction to laboratory science and ICT
Guided career exploration and subject counselling

Senior Secondary

Preparing for the World

Our SS programme is the most academically intensive phase of school life at Graffims. Students choose from three specialisation tracks — Sciences, Arts, or Commercials — and are prepared comprehensively for WAEC and NECO examinations.

Our dedicated SS teachers focus on exam technique, deep subject mastery, and university entrance preparation. Regular mock exams and personalised coaching ensure no student is left behind.

Three specialisation tracks: Sciences, Arts, Commercials
Full WAEC & NECO preparation and mock exams
University entrance (JAMB/UTME) coaching programme
Career counselling and post-school guidance
