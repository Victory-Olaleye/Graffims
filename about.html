Our Story

A Legacy Built on
Purpose & Passion

Graffims School was founded in 2012 by a Mr. and Mrs. Olaleye, educators who believed that quality education could transform communities. What began as a tutorial center to help children with academic needs has grown into a thriving institution of learners and dedicated staff members.

For over a decade, Grafims has remained steadfast in its commitment to nurturing the whole child academically, morally, and socially. Our graduates have gone on to conquer the world in their respective fields.

Today, Graffims School stands as a beacon of academic excellence in the region, combining modern teaching methods with strong traditional values to prepare students for a rapidly changing world.

5,000+ Students Enrolled since Inception

What Drives Us

Our Mission, Vision & Values

Our Mission

To provide a nurturing, inclusive, and challenging educational environment that equips every student with the knowledge, values, and skills needed to thrive in life and contribute meaningfully to society.

Our Vision

To be the leading institution in the region, producing globally competitive, morally upright, and innovative graduates who are equipped to lead positive change in every sector of society.

Core Values

Integrity, Excellence, Innovation, Respect, and Community. These are not just words on a wall — they are the principles that guide every decision, every lesson, and every relationship at Graffims School.
