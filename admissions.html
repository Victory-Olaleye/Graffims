Step by Step

The Admissions Process

Our admissions process is straightforward and transparent. Follow these steps to apply for a place at Graffims School.

01

Obtain Application Form

Collect the official admissions form from the school office during working hours (Monday–Friday, 8am–3pm), or download and print the form from this website. A non-refundable processing fee of ₦2,000 applies.

Forms available from May 5, 2025
02

Complete & Submit the Form

Fill out all sections of the form clearly and accurately. Attach all required supporting documents (see requirements below). Return the completed form to the Admissions Office before the closing date.

Submission deadline: June 30, 2025
03

Entrance Assessment

Eligible candidates will be invited to sit the Graffims School Entrance Assessment — a written exam covering English Language, Mathematics, and a General Aptitude paper. The exam takes approximately 2 hours.

Assessment date: July 12, 2025
04

Interview (Selected Candidates)

Shortlisted candidates may be invited for a brief interview with a member of the academic staff. This is an opportunity for us to learn more about the applicant and for families to ask questions about the school.

Interviews: July 19–21, 2025
05

Offer Letter & Enrolment

Successful candidates will receive an official offer letter by post and email. Parents must accept the offer and pay the acceptance fee within 10 working days to confirm their child's place. Failure to do so may result in the place being offered to another candidate.

Results released: July 28, 2025

What You Need

Entry Requirements

Requirements vary slightly by entry level. Please ensure all documents are ready before submitting your application.

Birth Certificate

Certified copy of birth certificate or a sworn affidavit of age from a magistrate's court. Child must be of the appropriate age for the class applied for.

Previous School Report

Most recent end-of-year school report card from the child's previous school, showing their performance in core subjects.

Passport Photographs

Four recent passport-sized photographs on a white background, taken within the last 3 months.

Birth Certificate

Certified copy of birth certificate or sworn affidavit of age. Applicants for JSS 1 must be between 10 and 13 years of age.

Primary School Leaving Certificate

Official Primary School Leaving Certificate (PSLC) or equivalent proof of completion of primary education.

Primary 6 Report Card

Final Primary 6 school report card showing performance across all subjects, especially English and Mathematics.

Birth Certificate

Certified copy of birth certificate or sworn affidavit. Applicants for SS 1 should typically be between 14 and 17 years of age.

BECE / JSS 3 Results

Official Basic Education Certificate Examination (BECE) results or JSS 3 final report card. A minimum of 5 credits is required for most tracks.

Subject Track Preference

A signed form indicating the applicant's preferred specialisation track — Sciences, Arts, or Commercials — subject to eligibility based on BECE results.
